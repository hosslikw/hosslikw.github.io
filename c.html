work. cv. ephemera. contact. posture.

Concrete

     

The choices made in the production of this focused on the formal qualities and the design was based on digitally rendered 3d objects. To achieve this affect the objects were painted in flat colors with subtle fades to imply false shadows. They are then coated in clear so when under light the eye struggles to determine the parameters of the forms. The shapes were made to mimic thoughts, becoming physical depictions of fantasies. The top of the undulating form is coated with concrete in order to anchor these otherwise hazy and soft fantasies in the room. To emphasize this motive two sets of initials are signed into the wet concrete much like those drawings seen in the sidewalks of American cities.
