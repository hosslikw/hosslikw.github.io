Robert Smith

Robert Smith takes its title from the one that is often cited as the most common name for a man in the English-speaking world. It takes the advertising form of the “wacky inflatable armed tube man” and codes it in a visual reference to a revered sculptural material, marble..

It looks down upon the viewer from behind a veil, staging a scene of judgment, shame, and amusement.
