About

My Friend Kyle Has a Brain Tumor "I have a friend named Kyle, who has a tumor between his brain and his skull. Kyle is an artist. I would like to tell you about Kyle and his art, how both are mutating. He’s not so different from us, after all; he’s also concerned with work and love."- Ben Nicholson http://www.brnart.com
