Uniform

In 2007 I was still trying to figure out if I had talent, or at least if I had more talent than the others around me. So I focused on something that was hard for others to access and fairly uncomfortable to do. Through multiple bouts of dehydration and an internship with a really talented glass blower Kiara Pelissier I found out that nope I don’t have it. In the process I made about 6 of these 2 and a half foot vessels. Honestly, an image of one of them is included here because I’m still proud of them. They sit in the homes of people I love and from time to time when I’ve had too much wine i'll try and clean them against the will of their owners.
