Heat form plastic, flourescent light, metal. 2016

Folded Sign

     

The form is based on the three bars of a 7-eleven sign which illuminates continuous convenience. Made from heat forming plastic it is comprised of 6 separate pieces that are riveted together and then reformed to make a hood or look like space vacated by a body underneath a blanket. This piece was accompanied by a presentation that detailed the economics of art making expressed with a manipulatable animation seen here.
